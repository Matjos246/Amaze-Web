WHO ARE WE

Amaze Interior and Exterior Nigeria Limited is a trusted name in the world of interior and exterior design, dedicated to transforming ordinary spaces into stunning, functional environments.

With years of experience and a passion for quality, we provide expert solutions in POP ceiling design, cement work, wooden window installations, 3D wall panels, interlock paving, TV feature walls, wallpaper installation, wall design, and tile laying.

Our team of skilled craftsmen and designers work closely with clients to bring their visions to life—whether for residential, commercial, or public spaces.

At Amaze, we combine creativity with professionalism, using only the best materials and techniques to ensure lasting beauty and durability in every project.

We are proudly Nigerian, proudly creative, and proudly committed to excellence. Let us help you create a space that truly amazes.

Our visions

We believe in enriching lives through design. This philosophy is reflected in our own work, in our design choices and in the suppliers and partners that we collaborate with. It is embedded in our strong conviction that an interior should balance aesthetics with layers of visual generosity. Central to our work are our clients – the people who work and live their lives in the spaces that we create for them. Our experienced interior designers are committed to responding to our clients’ brief and lifestyle, creating original, authentic and individual interiors that stand the test of time.We are equally defined by the result of our work and the experience we create in the process. Together we deliver creative, intelligent, practical and cohesive interiors – be they residential, office or hospitality projects.
